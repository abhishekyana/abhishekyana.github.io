Presense

I'm currently a PhD candidate at Carnegie Mellon University's McWilliams Center for Cosmology working with Prof. Sergey Koposov on searching globular clusters using Gaia data as well as other ground-based observations.

Past

Before that, I've worked with Prof. Tiziana Di Matteo on supermassive black holes in cosmological hydrodynamic simulation. I got MS and BS degrees in physics at National Taiwan University under supervision of Prof. Tzihong Chiueh on computational cosmology with quantum wave dark matter.
